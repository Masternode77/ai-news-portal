# Autonomous Desk Narrative Lede

Write a 2-3 paragraph lede for a Compute Current analyst article.

Rules:
- Start with a concrete tension, actor, market move, or infrastructure constraint.
- Do not start with the source publication name.
- Do not use "Commercially," or "Operationally,".
- Do not summarize the source before explaining why the signal matters.
- Keep the lede human-readable and specific.
