# Adjacent Signal

Use for AI business, app, model, or software stories that may touch infrastructure later but do not yet clear the core bar.

Public label: Adjacent Signal
Opening style: watchlist-led
Allowed sections: Why It Is Adjacent; Possible Infrastructure Link; Boundary; Watch Condition
Forbidden phrases: should read it through
Required evidence fields: protagonist, concrete_event
Good deck: A legal AI rollout belongs on the adjacent watchlist unless it changes enterprise platform deployment.
Bad deck: A legal AI rollout is an infrastructure signal because it uses AI.
