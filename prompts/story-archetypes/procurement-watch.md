# Procurement Watch

Use for supplier capacity, delivery schedules, vendor leverage, component qualification, and equipment queues.

Public label: Operator Alert
Opening style: buyer-led
Allowed sections: Procurement Signal; Supplier Leverage; Buyer Exposure; Delivery Metric
Forbidden phrases: confirms timing, site readiness, buyer commitment, or operating impact
Required evidence fields: concrete_event, watch_metric
Good deck: Procurement teams need delivery evidence before treating the update as usable capacity.
Bad deck: The next disclosure confirms timing, site readiness, buyer commitment, or operating impact.
