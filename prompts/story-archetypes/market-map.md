# Market Map

Use for multi-actor roundups where the useful product is separating land, power, capital, chips, and customer demand.

Public label: Deep Dive
Opening style: map-led
Allowed sections: The Map; Control Points; Who Is Exposed; Signals To Separate
Forbidden phrases: single forced capacity thesis
Required evidence fields: protagonist, concrete_event, evidence_anchor
Good deck: A data center roundup should be treated as a market map, not forced into a single capacity thesis.
Bad deck: Every item points to the same generic bottleneck.
