# Compute Current Launch Overview

Compute Current tracks the constraints behind the AI buildout: power, capacity, chips, cooling, cloud infrastructure, capital flows, and siting risk.

The launch v1 surface is built around local Compute Current analysis pages, not a generic source-link feed. Source-only items are separated into watch lanes when they do not support a full article.

## Launch Surface

- Homepage: intelligence board with live local analysis, lane structure, source attribution, and newsletter capture.
- Articles: source-backed local pages with deck, byline, attribution, optional At a Glance, article body, what to watch, bottom line, source link, AI-assisted disclosure, and tags.
- Discovery: sitemap, RSS, archive, and search index exclude quarantined/noindex items.
- Transparency: about, methodology, editorial policy, AI disclosure, and subscribe pages are live.

## Publication Boundary

Compute Current does not claim original reporting or investment advice. Articles are AI-assisted source-derived analysis and should remain tied to the cited source record.
