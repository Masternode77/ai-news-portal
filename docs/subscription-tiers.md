# Subscription Tiers

## Free

$0. Includes the live board, Daily AI Infrastructure Brief, and limited archive search.

## Pro

$39/month or $399/year. Includes full article access, weekly analyst memo, premium archive, market maps, and PDF briefing downloads.

## Team

$299/month for 5 seats. Includes Pro features, saved topics, team dashboard, shared watchlists, and Slack/Teams alerts placeholder.

## Enterprise

Contact sales. Includes custom seats, API/RSS, custom watchlists, monthly briefing call, custom research, invoice billing, and dedicated support.

## Access Policy

The site currently uses a soft paywall. Premium previews show upgrade CTAs, but public content is not fully blocked until authentication and billing are configured.
